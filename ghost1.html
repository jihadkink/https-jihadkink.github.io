Highlight Places

Let the archive begin

If you ever end up in Northern Ireland, I suggest stepping across the pillars in Giant’s Causeway.

If you’re ever in Perth, Aus Lucy’s Love Shack is also referred to as The Vault. They’re cool for rave style nights out without being at a full on rave.

I’m legit upset this Polish Techno club shut down when I only got the chance to go there once.

Shalimar Garden is located in Pakistan. Don’t know why you’d ever end up in Pakistan, buuuuut if you do, then I recommend seeing it at night.

Elysium Goth Nights at 24 Moons, owned by a guy named Sebastian, is my favourite regular event in all of Melbourne.

So, Poland has a Jesus statue. Technically, it is bigger than the one in Brazil.

If you like Bars with retro games like arcane machines and nintendo from before the 2000s and are in Melbourne, I highly recommend Bartronica
